Marwadi University Library becomes the FIRST EVER LIBRARY to have GUINNESS WORLD RECORD Library of Marwadi Education Foundation’s Group of Institutions, Rajkot has recently got place in Guinness World Record after successful completion of record attempt for relay reading which was executed under the title “Most People participating in a Reading Relay - Single Work.” “Gandhiji’s Autobiography: My experiments with Truth”. 3071 students and staff participated and read the aforesaid autobiography in relay successfully. The record attempt started at 8:00 am on 22nd September, 2015 and lasted till 6:30 pm. Guinness World Record authority has recently approved the record and declared MEFGI Library as Guinness World Record Holder. More details are available on official website of Guinness World Record.
